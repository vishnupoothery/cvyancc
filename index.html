Who are we :

Dr. Sukumari H. U.

Naturopathy and Yoga Physician, Dr. Sukumari H. U., BNYS., M S (Counselling and Psychotherapy) has completed her Bachelor of Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences from JSS Institute of Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences, Ooty under The TN Dr MGR Medical University, Chennai and Master of Science from Kuvempu University, Shimoga and practicing since 2011. She is also a Kannada writer of health related articles in magazines and deliver lectures to the general public on health.

Dr. Shrishailappa Badami

Director, Product Development and Publications, Dr. Shrishailappa Badami, M. Pharm., Ph D., is an expert in herbal formulations, their standardization, bioactivity, isolation of phytoconstituents, green chemistry and research methodology. He has received 97 lakhs research and related grants and guided 10 Ph Ds and 20 M Pharm. He has published more than 100 peer reviewed publications including 55 in international journals, 45 in national journals, six chapters in books, three review articles and 30 general articles. He has authored two Kannada books, Aarogya Kaipidi and YuvapeeLigeyalli Santaanaheenathe- RasaayanikagaLa Jagattu Manukulakke Aapattu, which are more popular in Karnataka. He is actively involved in popularizing healthy life style practices by delivering lectures to the general public.
